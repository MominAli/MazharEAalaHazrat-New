Quranic Verses
  • وَمِمَّا رَزَقْنَاهُمْ يُنفِقُونَ
    And the pious are those who spend in Our way from what We have given them.
  • خُذْ مِنْ اَمْوَالِهِمْ صَدَقَةً تُطَهِّرُهُمْ وَ تُزَكِّیْهِمْ بِهَا
    Take alms from their wealth; thereby you will cleanse them and purify them.
  • وَ الَّذِیْنَ هُمْ لِلزَّكٰوةِ فٰعِلُوْنَ
    And they who give Zakat are the ones who attain prosperity.
Hadith References
  • Musnad Ahmad: Wealth without Zakat will become a bald snake on Judgment Day.
  • Tabarani (Al-Awsat): Nations that withhold Zakat face famine.
  • Hazrat Ali (RA): The poor suffer due to the negligence of the rich.
Legal Rulings (Masā’il)
  1. Zakat cannot be given to disbelievers or certain sects. It will not be valid. (Fatāwā Razawiyyah Sharīf)
  2. Conditions: Must be Muslim, sane, and adult. (Bahār-e-Sharī‘at)
  3. Zakat must be given to a non-Hāshimi Muslim poor person. (Bahār-e-Sharī‘at)
  4. Lunar year (Hijri) is used to calculate Zakat. Temporary dips in wealth don’t cancel obligation. (Bahār-e-Sharī‘at)
  5. Zakat is Fardh. Denial is disbelief; neglect is sinful. (Bahār-e-Sharī‘at)
